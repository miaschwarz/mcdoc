User Stories

User Story 1

Writing a Review

-Arthur just visited The Valloway Terrace in London and wants to review his experience
-He goes to the search page to find it, clicks on the restaurant, then clicks "Write a Review"
-Arthur is able to create a title, rate it and write his review
-He then can go back to the restaurant page and find his review

User Story 2

Save places for an upcoming trip
-Julie is planning for her upcoming trip to Rome
-She starts on the search page in the section for Rome and uses the heart to save places
-She remembers her friend, Matt, telling her about a place he went to when he visited
-Julie searches his handle, goes to his visted restaurants, searches Rome, and finds "Tonnarello"

User Story 3

Look for a restaurant near you
-Rachel is looking where to eat on the map, she's near Covent Garden
-She sees on the map that her friend Monica went to a place a few blocks away but left it a 2.5
-But at the restaurant next door her friend Chandler left a review of 4.5 so she decides to go there


CONS OF FOODIE

  • You need at least 5-8 friends that are active on the app to really see the benefits otherwise you aren't getting as many reccomendations.
  • If your purpose is to use it to travel you need well-traveled friends or want to be the person who tries restaurants first.
